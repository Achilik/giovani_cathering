Our Story

Founded in 2025, Giovani Catering School was created from a deep passion for the art and science of cuisine. Our mission is to combine traditional techniques with modern innovation so students graduate ready for the global culinary landscape. We focus on hands-on training, professionalism, and creativity — shaping not only cooks, but confident culinary artists and business-savvy professionals.

We offer professional programs in pastry, international cuisine, food styling, and catering management — designed for industry readiness and real-world experience.
