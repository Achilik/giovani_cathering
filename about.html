Our Story

Founded in 2025, Gourmet Catering School began its legacy with its founders' passion for the art and science of cuisine. Our motto, "Chef Antoine Dublious' recipe for success," is more than a slogan—it's the driving force behind our dedication to nurturing future culinary masters. We emphasize a blend of traditional techniques and modern innovation, ensuring our students are prepared for the diverse demands of the global gastronomic landscape. Our goal is to cultivate not just cooks, but true culinary artists who can make a profound impact in the industry.
